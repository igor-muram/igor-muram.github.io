$$y'_{x}=\;$$ $$\frac{y'_{t}}{x'_{t}}=\;$$ $$\frac{e^{t}\left ( 1 - t \right )+e^{t}}{-e^{-t}\left ( 1 - t \right )+e^{-t}}=\;$$ $$\frac{e^{t}\left ( 2 - t \right )}{te^{-t}}$$
